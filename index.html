What is good design?

Good design understands its audience, is accessible, and maintains artistic authenticity. Audience and accessibility tend to go hand in hand. Designers that understand their audience know what visuals to use in order to appeal to the people that they want to. Without this aspect, design doesn't appeal to viewers, which is largely its job. Accessibility is similar, but takes it a step further with expanding the audience. Accessibility not only makes the design objectvely attainable for a designer's audience, but it extends a hand to those outside of the core audience, simply by making a design comprehensible. The final pillar is authenticity, which just means that good design is done with the right designer. Someone who is comfortable and happy with what they were tasked to do. Everyone has different tastes, so "good design" is going to be different depending on who's working on it.
